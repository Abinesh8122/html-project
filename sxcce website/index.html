Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide quality technical education with moral values and social responsibility.

Vision

To be a premier institution nurturing innovative engineers and leaders for a better tomorrow.

About Us

Established in 1998, SXCCE has been a pioneer in technical education with a focus on holistic development of students. Our college has consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in the region.

Objectives

  • To provide industry-relevant technical education
  • To foster research and innovation
  • To develop ethical professionals
  • To promote entrepreneurship
